Chroma Quest

Instructions

Welcome to Chroma Quest! Your goal is to mix colors to match the target color as closely as possible.

How to Play:

  1. A Target Color will be displayed.
  2. Choose colors from the palette below to mix. Click on a color button to add it to your mixture.
  3. Observe the Mixed Color and the Accuracy percentage.
  4. Use the Undo button to remove the last color added.
  5. Use the Reset button to clear your mixture and start over for the current level.
  6. Once you are satisfied with your mixture and the accuracy is high enough (85% or more), the Submit button will be enabled.
  7. Click Submit to advance to the next level with a new target color.
  8. Try to achieve perfect accuracy (100%)!
